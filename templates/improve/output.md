# Improve Output Format

The generated ADR should include the following sections:

## 1. Context
Brief description of the situation or recurring pattern that led to the decision.

## 2. Decision
Clear statement of the architectural decision made.

## 3. Consequences
Implications, benefits, and drawbacks of the decision.

## 4. Alternatives Considered
Other approaches that were considered and why they were not chosen.

---

**Example Structure:**

```markdown
# ADR: [Decision Title]

## Context
[Description of the situation or pattern]

## Decision
[Clear statement of the decision]

## Consequences
- [Benefit or drawback]
- [Another consequence]

## Alternatives Considered
- [Alternative approach]: [Reason not chosen]
- [Another alternative]: [Reason not chosen]
```
